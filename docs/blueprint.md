# **App Name**: Arreglos Express

## Core Features:

- Image Upload Form: Enable users to upload photos of their clothing needing alterations.
- Alteration Examples Display: Showcase a variety of alteration examples with before and after photos to inspire customers.
- WhatsApp Contact Button: Implement a direct WhatsApp button linking to +34611605751 for quick inquiries and service requests.
- Email Contact Form: Include a contact form for customers to send detailed inquiries or requests via email.
- AI-Powered Alteration Suggestion Tool: Provide a generative AI powered tool that suggests possible alterations based on the uploaded image. The LLM has reasoning capability and may not suggest a particular alteration if it won't improve the garment.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5), evoking trust and sophistication, in harmony with tailoring and style.
- Background color: Very light desaturated Indigo (#E8EAF6), offering a clean and professional canvas for the clothing images to stand out.
- Accent color: Violet (#7E57C2), used to highlight important calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif, chosen for its clear readability and neutral aesthetic, ensuring the focus remains on the clothing alterations.
- Use simple, elegant icons to represent different alteration types and service features.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout with generous spacing to present alteration examples and form elements clearly.
- Incorporate subtle animations, such as smooth transitions, to enhance user engagement.
